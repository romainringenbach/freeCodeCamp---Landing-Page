Introducing LF-11 tape recorder.

The flow of thoughts and ideas never stops in the complexity of our brain. How many times have we had something in mind, but we didn't take time to log somewhere, thus we regret forgetting it? What if we could record it, in the simplest way? For a long time, our history has been spoken, yet today's technology allows us to keep fragments of our voice, bringing us the possibility to probe our past. Let us introduce our new field recorder. From important speech to deep thoughts through the next start-up idea, it will never let you down on the field.

From big machines to your pocket.

Zero friction.

One button is all it takes to begin recording. Small enough to fit any pocket. Take it anywhere, record anywhere. Perfectly fitting the palm of your hand, the LF-11 stimulates intuitive muscle reflex control, allowing you to pilot the device even from your pocket. With its inputs and outputs, you register your voice through a mic on your shirt and listen to your voice with a headset while registering. The LF-11 also features a physical play controler allowing you to fast-forward or rewind. Addition buttons on the side give you the volume control.

Specifications.

Highlights

  • 1x stereo two-way 3.5 mm jack input/output/headset connectors
  • 1x stereo headphones 6.35 mm jack connector
  • internal microphone and speaker
  • 128 GB internal storage
  • 24-bit/96 kHz usb audio interface
  • bluetooth low energy
  • wifi
  • rechargeable battery
  • 11 hr battery life
  • 64x64 pixel colored display

Dimensons and weight

  • 100 mm x 60 mm x 16 mm
  • 170 g / 5.6 oz

Electrical characteristics

  • two-way jacks (3.5 mm TRRS)
  • Stereo input:
    • input impedance 9.5 kOhm
    • analog gain 0 - 42 dB
    • max level 8 dBu (2 Vrms)
    • SNR 105 dBA
  • Headset:
    • max level out 2 dBu (1 Vrms)
    • SNR 110 dBA
    • microphone SNR 98 dBA
    • headphone output (6.35 mm TRS)
    • max level 8 dBu (2 Vrms)
    • SNR 120 dBA
